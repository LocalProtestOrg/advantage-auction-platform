Platform Capabilities
Everything a real auction needs
Built from the ground up for professional auction operations — not adapted from a generic marketplace template.
🏷️
Proxy Bidding
Auto-increment bids up to a max. Transparent price resolution with anti-snipe lot extensions.
🛡️
Anti-Snipe Protection
Bids placed in the final 2 minutes automatically extend the lot close time — fair for all bidders.
📋
Seller Dashboard
Manage all your lots from a single view. Add, edit, and organize without leaving the page.
💳
Stripe Checkout
Integrated card payments with automatic invoice generation for every winning bid.
📄
Printable Invoices
Professional PDF-ready invoices for every purchase. Accessible anytime from the buyer dashboard.
📱
Mobile-First Design
Bidders and sellers can manage everything on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop.
🔒
Paddle Anonymity
Buyer identity is protected during bidding. Full address revealed only after verified payment.
📊
Operational Reports
Admin sees auction state, payment totals, and lot-level performance at a glance.
