How Shipping Works on Advantage.Bid

1

Browse shipping auctions

Find auctions marked "Ships Nationwide" on this page. Each card shows the number of shippable lots.

2

Bid on shippable lots

Inside each auction, individual lots marked with 📦 are available for nationwide shipping by the seller.

3

Win and complete payment

If you win, complete your secure online payment. Your shipping address is collected at checkout.

4

Receive your items

The seller arranges shipping after payment is confirmed. You receive tracking information once your items are shipped.
