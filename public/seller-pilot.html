Simple Process

From listing to payment in three steps

No auction house visits. No guesswork. Your items are online and bidding in minutes.

1

Submit your inventory

Upload photos and descriptions of what you're selling. Our team reviews and publishes your auction, handling the professional presentation.

2

Buyers compete online

Registered buyers bid competitively. Anti-sniping protection extends close times automatically so every item gets its full market price.

3

Collect and schedule pickup

Winning buyers pay securely online. You set the pickup window - buyers arrange collection directly. Simple, documented, done.
