You don’t have to run it — Advantage does. You create the auction and add your items from your phone; we publish it to buyers, run the live bidding with anti-sniping, close each lot, collect payment, and schedule pickup. Your job is to decide what to sell. Ours is everything after that. Start selling →

Whether it’s a restaurant closing, surplus equipment, or an entire inventory, you list each asset as a lot with photos and a short description. Advantage exposes it to buyers across the region, runs competitive bidding, and handles invoicing and payment automatically — so you convert assets to cash without holding a physical sale. See business liquidation →

Each item becomes its own lot with a starting bid. Registered buyers place bids during the auction window, and prices climb as they compete. A bid in the final two minutes extends that lot so everyone gets a fair chance. When a lot closes, the highest bidder wins, is invoiced and charged automatically, and schedules a pickup window. See the builder →

Inherited pieces often sell best when buyers can compete for them. Add each item — or group smaller pieces into lots — with a photo and short note, and let bidding find the true value.
