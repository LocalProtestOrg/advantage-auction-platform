Whether it’s a restaurant closing, surplus equipment, or an entire inventory, you list each asset as a lot with photos and a short description. Each item becomes its own lot with a starting bid. Registered buyers place bids during the auction window, and prices climb as they compete. A bid in the final two minutes extends that lot so everyone gets a fair chance. When a lot closes, the highest bidder wins, is invoiced and charged automatically, and schedules a pickup window.
