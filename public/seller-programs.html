Individual Seller

Selling personal property

Best for: families, estate executors, downsizing, moving, collectors, hobby sellers, and anyone selling personal property.

Need to sell items from a home, estate, collection, or life transition? The Individual Seller program is designed to make it simple. Create your account, build your lots with easy Smart Catalog Tools, and reach motivated online buyers. There is no seller platform fee or commission at launch, and our team helps guide you through your first auction.

  • No seller platform fee or commission at launch
  • Fast, self-service setup
  • Smart Catalog Tools to build lots quickly
  • Guided help through your first auction
Pricing: No seller platform fee or commission at launch. Approximately 3% payment-processing cost when your items sell.

Professional Seller

Running auctions as a business

Best for: auction companies, estate sale companies, professional liquidators, and businesses running recurring auctions.

Already selling professionally? The Professional Seller program adds operational flexibility designed for experienced auction businesses, including reserve pricing, custom starting bids, preview windows, and pickup scheduling on your own timeline. Program terms are tailored to how your business works, so let us talk through the details together.

  • Reserve pricing and custom starting bids
  • Preview windows for your buyers
  • Flexible pickup scheduling
  • Custom or negotiated program terms
Pricing: Custom or negotiated program terms, tailored to your business. Let's talk through the details.
