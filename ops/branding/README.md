## Brand Principles (Working)

These are working principles, not final brand guidelines:

**1. Managed, not automated.**
Advantage is a full-service auction operation, not a listing software.
Every touchpoint should feel like working with a professional team.

**2. Trustworthy, not salesy.**
Sellers are trusting Advantage with significant inventory, often under emotional
circumstances. Language and design should feel honest and measured, not promotional.

**3. Clear over clever.**
Copy should be direct and plainspoken. Estate executors and business owners
do not respond to clever ad copy — they respond to clarity about what they get.

**4. Professional, not corporate.**
The platform serves real people in real situations. Warm, professional language.
No jargon. No buzzwords.

## Current Visual Identity (Observed from Platform)

From the existing admin and widget pages, the platform currently uses:

| Element | Value |
|---|---|
| Primary blue | `#2563eb` |
| Dark background | `#1e293b` |
| Text | `#1e293b` |
| Subtext | `#64748b` |
| Border | `#e2e8f0` |
| Font | System UI / `-apple-system` |
| Border radius | 8-10px (cards), 6px (buttons) |

These are not yet formally documented as brand standards — they are observed
from the engineering implementation and subject to design review.
