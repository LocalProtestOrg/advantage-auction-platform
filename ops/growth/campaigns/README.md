## Campaign Design Principles

**1. Qualify before volume.**
Advantage operates a managed auction model — unqualified seller volume creates
operational overhead. Campaigns should pre-qualify leads by auction size,
item category, and pickup feasibility before routing them to the onboarding flow.

**2. Education first.**
Most estate executors and liquidators have never used an online auction platform.
Each campaign should lead with education (what the process looks like, what to
expect) before asking for commitment.

**3. Clear handoff to onboarding.**
Every campaign ends with a single CTA: begin the seller application. The
application is the onboarding funnel entry point — campaigns do not try to
close sellers themselves.

**4. No cold promises.**
Campaigns must not promise specific sale prices, timelines, or outcomes.
Advantage's legal and compliance obligation is accurate representation.
