## Page Sections

### Section 1: Who This Is For

- You're managing an estate and need to liquidate the contents
- You have furniture, collectibles, jewelry, tools, or household goods
- You want to maximize what you recover without a traditional estate sale

### Section 2: How the Process Works

**Step 1 — Submit your information**
Tell us about the estate: location, rough inventory size, and timing needs.
Takes about 5 minutes.

**Step 2 — We assess and schedule**
Our team reviews your submission and contacts you within [X business days]
to discuss the auction plan, pickup window, and timeline.

**Step 3 — We photograph and list**
Advantage handles all photography, lot creation, and listing.
You don't need to know anything about auction software.

**Step 4 — We run the auction**
Bidding opens online. Registered buyers bid competitively. You can track
progress in real time.

**Step 5 — Buyers pick up. You get paid.**
Buyers collect items during the scheduled pickup window.
You receive your payout after the buyer premium is collected.
