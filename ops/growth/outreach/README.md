Architecture and planning for direct outreach to prospective sellers and partners.
Includes lead sourcing, sequencing logic, routing, and CRM integration.

| Directory listing (estate sale companies) | Cold | Low — requires research |

---

## Outreach Operating Rules

1. **Opt-in only for marketing email.** Cold outreach to business contacts may
   comply with CAN-SPAM / CASL if legitimately interest-based, but all campaign
   email must include unsubscribe. This is not optional.

2. **Qualify before outreach.** Do not send volume outreach to unqualified lists.
   Each batch should have clear qualification criteria defined before send.

3. **No guaranteed promises in outreach copy.** No claims about specific sale prices,
   timelines, or outcomes that operations cannot consistently deliver.

4. **Track every batch.** Each outreach campaign is logged with: date sent,
   list size, source, subject line, open rate, reply rate, applications generated.
